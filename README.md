# 📉 Tech Layoffs Analysis: AI, Revenue, and Workforce Impact

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes recent tech layoffs to understand how workforce reductions relate to **AI adoption, company revenue, and financial performance**.

The goal is to move beyond simple counts and answer:

* Are layoffs associated with positive financial signals?

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze trends in tech layoffs over time
* Compare **AI-cited vs non-AI layoffs**
* Evaluate **layoff severity (% workforce cut)**
* Explore relationships between layoffs and:

  * company revenue
  * stock market reaction
  * estimated cost savings

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Source: Tech Layoffs Tracker (2026)
* Size: ~28 companies × 26 features

---

## 🧠 Methods Used

* Pandas for data manipulation
* Seaborn & Matplotlib for visualization
* Groupby aggregations and correlation analysis
* Time-series feature engineering

---
